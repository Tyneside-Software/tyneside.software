How we create jobs in Tyneside

While we’re building the core logistics and work-management software, our team will also build professional websites for local businesses.

Website revenue → junior roles

Every website we create for a paying customer directly funds real software engineering jobs — starting at the very bottom. We deliberately take on people just entering the workforce (or re-entering it). We train them from the ground up, give them real client work, and recycle every pound of website revenue straight back into more junior roles.

Our own sites are the proof

Our own websites — this one, the charity site, the cleaner portal — are all built in-house. They serve as living proof of what we can deliver. Not just writing software: a pathway that turns local talent into skilled engineers, one website and one junior role at a time.
